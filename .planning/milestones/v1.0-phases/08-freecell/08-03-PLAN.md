  <verify>Human approval after testing all verification steps below</verify>
  <done>FreeCell approved as playable and visually correct on tablet viewport</done>
  <what-built>Complete FreeCell game with tap/double-tap/drag interaction, auto-foundation with fly animation, shake on invalid moves, seeded deals with restart, undo, win celebration, and settings</what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    1. Start local server: `cd /home/trashdev/projects/congames && python3 -m http.server 8080`
    2. Open Chrome at localhost:8080, enable DevTools device emulation: Custom 2000x1200, landscape, touch enabled
    3. Click "FreeCell" from welcome screen

    **Layout Check:**
    4. Verify 4 free cell slots visible top-left (dashed outline when empty)
    5. Verify 4 foundation slots visible top-right (faint suit symbols when empty)
    6. Verify center area shows deal number ("Spel #N"), undo/restart/new game/settings buttons
    7. Verify 8 tableau columns below with all cards face-up
    8. Verify first 4 columns have 7 cards, last 4 have 6 cards
    9. Verify cards are readable -- can distinguish rank and suit clearly
    10. Verify all tap targets are >= 56px

    **Basic Moves:**
    11. Tap a card at bottom of a column -- verify it highlights (selected)
    12. Tap a valid destination column -- verify card moves
    13. Tap a card then tap an empty free cell -- verify card moves to free cell
    14. Tap a card in a free cell then tap a valid column -- verify card moves back

    **Multi-Card Moves:**
    15. Build a small descending alternating-colour sequence in a column
    16. Tap the top card of the sequence -- verify entire sequence highlights
    17. Move sequence to valid destination -- verify all cards move together
    18. Try moving a sequence that exceeds the (freeCells+1) x 2^emptyCols limit -- verify it fails with shake animation

    **Auto-Foundation:**
    19. Move an ace to a foundation (tap ace, tap foundation slot) -- verify it goes
    20. Play until auto-foundation triggers naturally -- verify cards visibly fly to foundation (~200ms animation)
    21. After auto-foundation, tap undo -- verify auto-foundation AND the player's move are both reversed

    **Double-Tap Auto-Move:**
    22. Tap an ace that hasn't moved yet -- it highlights
    23. Tap the same ace again (double-tap) -- verify it auto-moves to foundation
    24. Select a card with no valid foundation move -- double-tap should try free cell

    **Invalid Moves:**
    25. Try an invalid move -- verify shake animation on selected card(s), no sound, card deselects

    **Deal Number & Restart:**
    26. Note the deal number in header (e.g., "Spel #54321")
    27. Tap Herbegin (restart) -- verify same deal number and same card layout
    28. Tap new game -- verify different deal number

    **Undo and Settings:**
    29. Make several moves, then tap undo repeatedly -- verify cards return correctly
    30. Open settings -- toggle timer/moves off, verify they hide from display
    31. Tap back button -- verify return to welcome screen

    **Win (if achievable):**
    32. If possible, play to win -- verify celebration animation triggers with deal number shown

    Expected: Game plays smoothly, matches MS FreeCell feel, readable cards, comfortable tap targets
